Welcome to THERASSISTANT

This tool is designed to help identify the services you provided during the session and guide you through documenting them appropriately.
You do not need to know billing codes to use this tool.

You will first answer questions about what occurred during the session. Based on your responses, THERASSISTANT will identify which services may apply and generate a coding report.

After the coding report is generated, THERASSISTANT will provide a coding audit summary and recommended next documentation tools.
