Best layout rule:

Charge Capture, Claims, Denials, Payments, and Patient Billing should all look familiar, but each queue should change the center of attention.

Charge Capture centers on documentation and coding.
Claims centers on submission and clearinghouse status.
Denials center on CARC/RARC and appeal strategy.
Payments center on ERA matching and reconciliation.
Patient Billing centers on balances, statements, and collections.
